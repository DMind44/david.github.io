Hello! My name is David Mindlin, and I am a current Master's student at USC Games. After completing my Bachelor's in Computer Science at Harvey Mudd College, I worked at Datadog creating integrations for AWS services. Prior to graduation, I interned multiple summers at Meta, working on core infrastructure teams. With a strong background in programming and computer science, I am fully equipped to meet the meet the technical demmands of games projects. Since starting at USC, I have gained experience working in game engines, as well and built skills in prototyping and ideation of games concepts. Currently, I am volunteering on an MFA thesis game as an engineer. Additionally, I strengthened my skills in user experience and game usability research, gaining hands-on experience in various projects.

Personally, I am a fan of puzzle games, roguelikes, and esports. Outside of games, I love animation, vocal performance, and improv.
